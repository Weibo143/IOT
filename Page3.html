Summary And Data

How ICT helped the Trading sector during Covid-19

In this investigation I will be discussing the TRAIDING sector and how we as a country and how the rest of the world got through the world wide pandemic with all of the new innovations that would not of happened if the Covid-19 virus did not happen. This PAT will focus on the different types of innovations that were created on an international level and the outcome of Covid-19.

From the research and results gathered by the survey, most of the respondents have an idea of what ICT is and its impact on the Trading Sector.

In the Survey of 25 People, this graph shows us what type of social platform they used during the lockdown period in order to keep in Contact with work, friends or family. We can see that most people used zoom in this case.

People who have a deeper understanding of ICT have a higher chance in succeeding in the future, 14 people know what ICT is where only 6 of the 25 don't know what's happening.

When Working from home it is crucial to have a working internet connection so that you can communicate and work with your Co-workers, Family and Friends.
